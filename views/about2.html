THE STORY BEHIND COMPANY

jsreport project was born as the reaction to the lack of customizable and open reporting tools on the market in 2013. The quick prototype was followed by the first official release in spring 2014. The original concept based on javascript templating engines and pdf converters has been proven in time by more than 80 000 downloads. The project is now matured and developed by the company located in the middle of the Europe, Prague, Czech Republic.
